Artist Statement

The Nile runs past my window

I grew up in a village in Minya, Upper Egypt — where the river is not a metaphor but a neighbor. Where ancient walls speak in three languages at once: Pharaonic, Islamic, and Coptic.

I studied fine arts at Assiut University, but I have always painted from instinct before theory. I am drawn to heritage — not as nostalgia, but as something living.

My style is not finished yet — and that is exactly what makes every painting different from the last. Everything I paint begins here.

"The Nile flows past my window. I grew up in a village in Minya, Upper Egypt — where the river is not a metaphor, but a neighbor."
